# Privacy Policy — Grasp

**Last updated:** 2026

## Summary

Grasp processes camera and image data **on your device**. We do **not** collect personal data on our servers for this app.

## Camera

- The camera is used to **capture** scenes and text for **local** analysis and storage.  
- Video frames are not uploaded to Grasp-operated servers.

## Local processing

- Inference and storage use **on-device** frameworks (including MLX Swift and SwiftData).  
- Your captures remain on your iPhone unless **you** share or export them.

## Data we do not collect

- We do **not** require an account for core Grasp features.  
- We do **not** sell your data.  
- We do **not** embed third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in the base app definition (verify each release).

## Third parties

- Apple system frameworks (SwiftUI, Vision, Natural Language, etc.) operate under Apple’s policies.  
- Optional model downloads may use Apple’s network stack; content is not sent to Grasp for profiling.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the **contact** link in the App Store listing or your support channel.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected here and in the App Store **Privacy Nutrition** labels as required.
