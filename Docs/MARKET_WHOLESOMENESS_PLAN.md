# Market Wholesomeness Plan — Grasp V1.0

Growth and sustainability **without** breaking core commitments: air-gap, no subscriptions, hardware respect, one-time pricing.

---

## 1. Executive summary

Grasp proves that **useful on-device AI** can ship without surveillance or recurring rent. The main tension is economic: **lifetime revenue is capped per user**, so sustainability depends on **organic acquisition**, **high-trust professional niches**, **optional one-time add-ons** (domain formats, not “Pro tiers”), and **volume licensing** for teams. This document aligns product, governance, and messaging so scale does not force ethical drift.

---

## 2. Sustainable revenue architecture

### Recommended: domain add-ons + team licensing

**Core app:** **$39.99** lifetime (unchanged) — full capture, VLM extraction, vault, search, updates as shipped.

**One-time domain add-ons** (illustrative; App Store mechanics and review must match Apple rules):

| Add-on | Audience | Indicative price | Value |
|--------|----------|------------------|-------|
| Legal Pack | Lawyers | $19.99 | Matter/client metadata, privilege-oriented export templates |
| Medical Pack | Healthcare | $19.99 | Safer local tagging patterns, structured export for approved workflows |
| Engineering Pack | Field / CAD | $14.99 | Measurement-oriented prompts and export helpers |
| Research Pack | Academia | $9.99 | Citation-oriented extraction helpers |

**Rule:** Add-ons are **formats and workflows**, not “smarter AI only for pay.” Core extraction quality stays the same for everyone.

**Grasp for Teams (volume licensing):**

- 5–24 seats: example **$34.99** per seat (adjust with real COGS and support load).
- 25–99 seats: example **$29.99** per seat.
- 100+ seats: custom pricing and IT deployment notes (e.g. internal model distribution policies).

**Optional:** **Grasp Foundation** — non-profit or fiscal sponsorship for grants (privacy / digital sovereignty). Requires separate legal setup.

### Rejected (and why)

| Idea | Why reject |
|------|------------|
| Freemium with scan limits | Creates artificial scarcity; fights “hardware respect” narrative |
| Subscription “Pro” | Breaks lifetime promise |
| Any Grasp-operated cloud sync | Destroys provable air-gap story |
| Ads or data monetization | Violates privacy and trust |
| Growth-at-all-costs VC | Conflicts with slow, careful roadmap |

## 4. 2026–2030 technical roadmap (milestones)

| Window | Focus |
|--------|--------|
| **Q1–Q2 2026** | Launch stability; bounded inference queue; model ID from Info.plist; more tests around actors and parsing |
| **Q3–Q4 2026** | Optional domain packs; Shortcuts (local-only); handwriting and export polish |
| **2027** | Spreadsheet/diagram-friendly exports; iPad layouts; batch flows with thermal safety |
| **2028** | Deeper automation (Siri Shortcuts, URL schemes) where local-only |
| **2029–2030** | New Apple hardware classes; MLX major versions; optional **open core** (schemas + MLX helpers), not a cloud pivot |

## 5. Growth channels (tactics and CAC)

| Channel | Tactic | CAC expectation |
|---------|--------|-----------------|
| **Content** | “The air-gapped office” — honest posts on local ML, thermals, privilege / compliance angles | Low (organic) |
| **Advocacy** | Optional donation match to orgs users trust (legal/compliance review first) | Low–medium |
| **Professional** | Short talks for legal / security / IT on **local** capture for sensitive docs | Medium (high trust) |
| **Academic** | Citations on on-device efficiency (thermal-aware inference, memory bounds) | Low (credibility) |

---

## 6. Risk mitigation (ethical drift)

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Pressure to add “just optional” cloud | **Founder's pledge** + public rejections in issues |
| Underpricing race | Compete on **trust and proof**, not cheapest price |
| Acquisition by surveillance-ad business | Pledge + community awareness; legal tools are limited—be transparent |
| Maintainer burnout | Narrow scope, automation, succession language |

---

## 7. Success metrics (beyond revenue)

| Metric | Example target |
|--------|----------------|
| Organic / direct traffic share | High vs paid |
| App Store rating | Stable high rating with substantive reviews |
| Support load per 1k users | Low and falling as docs improve |
| Repeat professional use | Vault depth, not vanity DAU |
| Third-party audits | At least one published summary when feasible |
