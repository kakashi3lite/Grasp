## GRASP FOUNDER'S PLEDGE

As the creator of Grasp, I commit to the following principles in perpetuity:

### Privacy

- Grasp will never upload your documents to any server I control.
- Grasp will never require an account.
- Grasp will never include third-party tracking or analytics.

### Pricing

- The core Grasp app will remain a one-time purchase, forever.
- There will never be a subscription tier.
- There will never be "cloud sync" as a paid feature.

### Hardware

- Grasp will always respect your device's thermal state.
- Grasp will never exploit your hardware to the point of degradation.
- Grasp will always prioritize efficiency over speed when necessary.

### Openness

- The core extraction schema will remain documented and accessible.
- If I can no longer maintain Grasp, the source code will be released to users (see succession plan in [`COMMUNITY_GOVERNANCE.md`](COMMUNITY_GOVERNANCE.md)).
- Grasp will not be sold to a company whose primary business is mass collection or monetization of personal data.
